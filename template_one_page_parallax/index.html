About Us

We are a diverse team from all around the world who want to help others find the same team chemistry and success we have.

Our Story

The four of us founders were united through our shared passion for entrepreneurship. On the surface, we have little in common, coming from four different countries, studying four different subjects, and having four different mindsets. But therein lies our strength: our diversity of experience, thought, and opinion. Having each spent far too much time working in underperforming teams, we are tired of seeing talent fail to produce results. We want every team to find the camaraderie and success we have, so we founded Emplow.

Our team is successful because we each have unique strengths and personalities that complement one another. We believe that successful teams don’t have to be an anomaly, because we can create them. We help our clients better understand their existing and prospective employees’ personalities, goals, and strengths, giving them the insight to create teams far greater than the sum of their parts.
